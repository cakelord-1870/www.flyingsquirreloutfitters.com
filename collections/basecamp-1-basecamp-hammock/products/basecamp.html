    “WOW” the Kids and Your Friends, Hang with Them All in 1 Hammock!

    Yes, one or two people can go off hammocking by themselves. But what of hammocking with ALL of your friends? Scoop up the dog! Invite the mailman! BaseCamp is a hammock experience for everyone.

    String it up at a picnic! Bring it to the park! Toss it up in the backyard for a super fabulous "staycation" with the fam. Fill it up at farmers markets, summer camps, beach parties, camping trips, anywhere that people gather and connect.

    BaseCamp is 21ftx12ft monstrosity of a hammock, designed for group hammocking in community spaces. First created as a "party favor" for music festivals, then launched and successfully funded on Indiegogo reaching 250% of our goal. This hammock is a party in itself, a portable gathering space pulling people together in the spirit of inclusion. Spice up your next party with the BaseCamp "Party Hammock", and we dare you to try and keep the kids out of this thing! Hammock with 600lbs of your closest friend and family.

     

    Supporting At-Risk Woman in Rural Thailand

    Each handcrafted hammock helps provide a sustainable living wage for stay-at-home seamstresses in Northern Thailand at risk of exploitation in the garment industry. We pride ourselves and handcrafted quality and avoid mass factory production - handmade with love! We stand by our promise of exceptional quality and guarantee against all manufacture defects.
